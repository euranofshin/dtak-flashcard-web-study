Option to participate in flashcard section

You have the option in participating in the flashcard section, where you will try to learn 100 flashcards over several rounds. Benefits of participating:

  • The flashcards are taken from a relevant Chinese textbook, [ref]
  • If you learn all the cards before the hour is up, you can take the exit survey early.
  • If you participate, you will receive insights about your learning style and flashcard learning with respect to other participants.

Your studying will happen in rounds. Each round is approximately 7 minutes and includes:

  • Three minutes of review
  • Three minutes of quizzing
  • Brief questions about how you feel
  • You choose whether to do another session. Remember, you only get insights about your performance if you participate in sessions until the exit survey.
