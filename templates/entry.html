Flashcard Section

Next is the flashcard section, where you will learn 100 flashcards over several rounds. Benefits of participating:

  • This is an opportunity to learn Chinese. The vocabulary is from the Elementary Chinese I textbook.
  • If you learn all the cards before the hour is up, you can take the exit survey early.
  • If you study until the exit survey time, you will receive insights about your learning style and how you compare to other participants.

Your studying will happen in rounds. Each round is approximately 7 minutes and includes:

  • Three minutes of review
  • Three minutes of quizzing
  • You choose whether to do another session. Remember, you only get insights about your performance if you participate in sessions until the exit survey.

The exit survey will be administered one hour from now; the proctor will have the time on the board. As a reminder, you will be paid when you take the exit survey. You are welcome to leave and come back for the exit survey.
