Detailed Information

What is the purpose of this research?

Our research team aims to assess and improve the effectiveness of AI-driven personalization on digital platforms for behavior change. Examples of the interventions we are considering include personalized push notifications on a health app. We consider the personalization to be successful if it increases long-term engagement – which we measure as app retention and performance on the goals multiple weeks after the app was first downloaded.

In order to personalize effectively, we want to understand how people think about long-term rewards, such as learning a new language. We also want to understand how people think about short-term rewards, such as skipping studying. If we understand how people think about and decide between short and long term rewards, we can design better practices for helping people prioritize the long-term. In this study, we have set up a gamified system to estimate how you think about short-vs-long term rewards.
